### About HIZE
A flagship IEEE Computer Society initiative bringing together innovation, technology, and academic excellence. At the IEEE High Impact Zonal Events (HIZE), we don't just showcase the future ‚Äî we create it. The stage is now set for our high-impact Zonal Events 2.0, led by **Abhinav Gambhir, Associate Director at Oracle**.

Every edition of HIZE is built on a foundation of excellence, inclusivity, and innovation. From expert-led workshops and panel discussions to dynamic hackathons and startup showcases, the series cultivates a rich environment for skill development and networking.

Backed by the global legacy of IEEE and the forward-thinking vision of the IEEE Computer Society, HIZE has become one of India's most anticipated student-driven technology movements.
